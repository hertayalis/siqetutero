Gigi Hadid is ready for fall. The Guest in Residence founder has released her new autumn collection, and took to social media to model a perfect layered look from the drop.

Yesterday, the model shared photos on Instagram of herself posing in the ensemble in a bathroom. In the first shot, she is pictured brushing her teeth in front of the mirror while wearing a teeny black triangle bra and matching cozy cardigan.

In the second, she is seen talking on an old-school pastel-yellow house phone while sitting on top of a toilet. She wears the same bra and sweater, plus tan wide-leg cashmere pants, baby-pink socks, and black leather loafers.
