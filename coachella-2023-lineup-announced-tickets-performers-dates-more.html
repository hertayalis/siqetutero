Coachella has done it once again.

The annual Music and Arts Festival held at Indio, CA’s Empire Polo Field has delivered yet another impressive and eclectic lineup that will surely please fans of all genres over its two weekends on April 14-16 and April 21-23.

On night one for both weekends, reggaeton legend Bad Bunny will be joined by Gorillaz, Burna Boy, The Chemical Brothers, Blondie and…Golden Globe nominee Idris Elba.

Yes, you read that right.

“The Wire’s” Stringer Bell will perform at Coachella this year.

Next up, K-Pop girl group BlackPink is headlining both Saturdays alongside Rosalia, Eric Prydz, boygenius, Charli XCX and The Kid LAROI.

Both weekends are set to close with elusive singer-songwriter Frank Ocean in the top spot. Before he takes the stage, Bjork, Kali Uchis, Porter Robinson, A Boogie Wit Da Hoodie and Dominic Fike will warm the crowds up for the “Channel Orange” icon.

Hit-making DJ Calvin Harris has also been tapped to “return to the desert.”

Now, that is a heckuva lineup.
