Biography

LaToya Thomas was born in Greenville, MS on July 6, 1981. A professional basketball player who starred at Mississippi State University and was selected first overall in the 2003 WNBA Draft by the Cleveland Rockers. During her career at Mississippi State, she was a 2-time AP SEC Player of the Year, 3-time AP All-SEC team selection, and a 4-time Kodak All-American. She was taken two picks ahead of Cheryl Ford in the 2003 WNBA Draft. She was named Mississippi’s Player of the Year during both her junior and senior years of high school.
