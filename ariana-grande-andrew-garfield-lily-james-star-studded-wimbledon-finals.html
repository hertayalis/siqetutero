See Ariana Grande, Andrew Garfield, and More Attend Star-Studded Wimbledon Finals

June 2024 · 2 minute read

A star-studded collection of tennis fans were in attendance for today's highly anticipated Wimbledon men's finals.

Several A-listers made their way to the All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club for the championship match between Spain's Carlos Alcaraz and Serbia's Novak Djokovic, where the stars' reactions to the spirited match. In a rare public outing, Ariana Grande was seen chatting with her seatmates, Andrew Garfield and Bridgerton actor (and the singer's Wicked co-star) Jonathan Bailey.

The "Thank U, Next" singer's casual ensemble consisted of a short-sleeved, high-neck sweater, a flowy maxi-length skirt in the same gray hue, and a classic tan trench coat, which she layered on later in the match. She added black pointed-toe pumps and a white cap sporting the Wimbledon logo, as well as small pearl and gold hoop earrings by Mejuri, and light azure sunglasses by Tiffany & Co. Meanwhile, both Garfield and Bailey looked handsome in tan blazers.

Karwai Tang//Getty Images

Other animated fans seated nearby include Lily James, Tom Hiddleston and his fiancée Zawe Ashton, model Poppy Delevingne, and Nick Jonas and his mother-and-law Madhu Chopra. James and Delevingne were photographed sharing similar passionate reactions to an upset during the match, with the Pam & Tommy actress wearing a navy blazer over a striped blouse, while the model wore a white satin bomber jacket.

Karwai Tang//Getty Images

Also seated nearby were Emma Watson and her brother Alex Watson, returning to the tournament after attending the women's singles final yesterday. The actress wore a white sleeveless top with a high neck and pointed oversized lapels, while her brother paired a salmon-toned suit jacket with a white button down.

Karwai Tang//Getty Images

Other attendees at the match included Brad Pitt, Daniel Craig, Rachel Weisz, Hugh Jackman, Barbie actor Ncuti Gatwa, and rapper Stormzy.

In addition to the selection of Hollywood stars, the Wales family also made their return to the royal box for the tournament's final match. In addition to Prince William and Princess Kate, who looked lovely in an emerald-green Roland Mouret sheath dress, Prince George made his second-ever appearance at the tournament, while Princess Charlotte attended the famed event for the first time. The two elder Wales kids coordinated in blue looks, and were photographed enjoying the thrilling game.

Karwai Tang//Getty Images
