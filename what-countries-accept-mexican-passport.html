What countries accept Mexican passport?

As a Mexican passport holder, you have the privilege of traveling to numerous countries across the globe without the requirement of a visa or with the ease of obtaining one upon arrival. The Mexican passport grants you access to a vast array of countries, making it a valuable travel document. Below, you will find a comprehensive list of countries that accept the Mexican passport:

United States

The United States, our neighboring country, allows Mexican passport holders to enter either visa-free or obtain a non-immigrant visa easily. However, it’s important to note that there are specific requirements and restrictions that may apply depending on your purpose of visit, such as tourism, work, or study.

Canada

Similar to the United States, Canadian immigration regulations offer Mexican passport holders the convenience of visiting the country without needing a visa. Certain conditions may apply based on the duration of your stay and the reason for your visit.

European Union

If you plan to explore the enchanting cities and picturesque landscapes of Europe, your Mexican passport is your gateway to the entire European Union (EU). With the Schengen visa exemption, you can visit countries like France, Germany, Italy, Spain, and many others without the hassle of obtaining a visa.

Latin American Countries

Being part of Latin America, your Mexican passport allows you to venture into other countries within the region without the need for extensive visa processes. Popular travel destinations such as Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Costa Rica, Peru, and Uruguay warmly welcome Mexican passport holders.

Asia

If you’re seeking to explore the vibrant cultures and ancient wonders of Asia, you’ll be pleased to know that several countries accept the Mexican passport. Destinations like Japan, South Korea, Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, and the Philippines offer visa-free or visa-on-arrival options for Mexican citizens.

Africa

Discover the majestic landscapes, diverse wildlife, and rich cultural heritage of Africa with your Mexican passport. Countries like Morocco, Tunisia, Seychelles, and Mauritius welcome Mexican travelers, either with visa exemptions or visa issuance upon arrival.

Middle East

If you’re intrigued by the intriguing blend of ancient traditions and modern marvels in the Middle East, your Mexican passport opens doors to countries like Israel, Qatar, United Arab Emirates, and Jordan. Each country may have different visa requirements and regulations, so it’s essential to check before your travel.

Oceania

Looking to explore the stunning natural wonders of Australia and New Zealand? Mexican passport holders can visit these countries under a visa waiver program, allowing for hassle-free travel. However, it’s vital to comply with specific entry requirements and stay limitations.

Other Countries

Aside from the regions and countries mentioned above, Mexican passport holders can also travel to various nations worldwide, including Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Egypt, Fiji, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Madagascar, Netherlands, Portugal, Russia, Sweden, Switzerland, Turkey, and many more.

  • Ensure you check the current visa requirements and immigration rules of your intended destination before your trip.
  • Always carry your valid passport and other necessary travel documents during your journey.
  • Remember that visa policies may change, so it’s recommended to consult the respective embassy or consulate of the country you plan to visit.

With your Mexican passport, the world becomes your oyster, offering a plethora of opportunities to indulge in diverse cultures, explore breathtaking landscapes, and create unforgettable memories. Embrace the freedom your passport grants you and embark on exciting journeys without unnecessary travel obstacles.
