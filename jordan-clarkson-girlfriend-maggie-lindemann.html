Jordan Clarkson Girlfriend Maggie Lindemann Is A Musician

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Jordan Clarkson’s girlfriend, Maggie Lindemann, is a talented singer and songwriter. 

They started dating in the summer of 2022, shortly after her previous relationship with singer Brandon Arreaga ended in May of the same year.

The couple has been supportive of each other careers, showcasing their strong bond, and are never shy to show their affection. 

Jordan Taylor Clarkson, born on June 7, 1992, is a Filipino-American basketball player. He currently plays for the Utah Jazz in the National Basketball Association (NBA).

Back in the 2014 NBA draft, the Washington Wizards initially selected him as the 46th overall pick in the second round, but he was soon traded to the Los Angeles Lakers.

Due to his impressive performance as a rookie, he earned a well-deserved spot on the NBA All-Rookie First Team.

Jordan was traded to the Cavaliers in 2018 and to the Utah Jazz in December 2019. 

Throughout his career, the athlete consistently showcased his skills and made significant contributions on the court, earning widespread recognition.

Furthermore, his efforts culminated in his being honored as the NBA Sixth Man of the Year in 2021.

Who Is Maggie Lindemann? Jordan Clarkson Girlfriend 

Margaret Elizabeth Lindemann was born into a German-Scottish family in July 1998 in Dallas, Texas.

Her journey into the music world started with sharing her singing recordings on the social media app Keek, which garnered her a growing following on various social media platforms.

One pivotal moment in her career came when her manager, Gerald Tennison, discovered a video of her singing on her Instagram page, leading to the beginning of her music career.

To pursue her dreams further, she made the decision to move to Los Angeles.

Maggie gained widespread recognition with her breakout single “Pretty Girl” in 2016, which achieved notable chart success in Sweden, Ireland, the UK, and the Netherlands.

Over the years, she has evolved her musical style, transitioning into pop rock with the release of her debut EP, “Paranoia,” in 2021.

Following her musical journey, Maggie released her debut album, “Suckerpunch,” on September 16, 2022.

Surprisingly, in 2016, Lindemann bravely came out as bisexual, embracing her identity publicly. 

Maggie Lindemann Has Found Herself In Controversies

In 2015, a video leaked on Tumblr involving Lindemann’s then-boyfriend, Carter Reynolds, a Vine star, and her. 

In the video, he was seen pressuring her into engaging in explicit activities, despite her repeated expressions of discomfort and unease.

Likewise, during a performance in Malaysia in 2019, Lindemann encountered legal trouble.

She was taken off stage and arrested for not having a professional visit pass, a requirement for foreigners working in the country.

The event organizers acknowledged their mistake, and she was released on bail the following day.

Lindemann referred to the experience as “five days of living hell.” 

Jordan Clarkson Committed To Play For Gilas At World Cup

SBP president Al S. Panlilio confirmed this exciting development to the Inquirer on Tuesday.

Clarkson holds dual citizenship with the Philippines and the United States, with his Filipino citizenship being derived from his ancestral lineage.
