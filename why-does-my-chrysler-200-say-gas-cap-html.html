May 2024 · 5 minute read

Answer

If your Check Fuel Cap light illuminates, it’s possible that you simply neglected to replace the cap on the tank. The vehicle will not recognise that the gasoline cap is on if it is not entirely fastened.

How can I turn off my gas cap light?

How to Replace a Loose or Missing Gas Cap Warning Light

Your vehicle’s engine should be turned off. If your vehicle is equipped with a locking gas cap door, press the button to unlock it.

To get to the gas cap door, take a step forward. Open the door.

Remove the gas cap and replace it.

Close the door to the gas cap.

If the warning light does not go away, use an OBD-II code scanner.

Where is the gas cap on a Chrysler 200, for example? 

On the inside of the car, there is no release button or lever for the gas tank door. Simply locate the gasoline door in the vehicle’s rear compartment and press it inward. The door will pop open, and you’ll be able to get to the gas cap.

Will the check engine light turn off if the gas cap is tightened?

If the light was caused by a loose gas cap, the check engine light should turn off after a few minutes of driving. After the check engine light has gone off, pay attention to the dashboard. If the light keeps coming on and then going off after you tighten the gas cap, it’s because it’s too loose.
